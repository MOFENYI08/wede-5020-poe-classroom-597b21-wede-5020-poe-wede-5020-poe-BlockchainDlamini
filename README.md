## Project Overview

.Name of the organisation: Melanin Barbie’s hair salon.  
.Brief history: The salon is about a talented, young woman. Who knows how to braid and wanted to pursue her talent by opening a salon. It is not just a talent but a hobby for the young women. The salon was established in 2022 by Lethabo Mofenyi Rakobelwa, when she was doing grade 9, in a place called Hammanskraal specifically in Kanana, in a city called Pretoria, in a province called Gauteng, and a country called South Africa. The salon was bought by her mother, wanting to see her daughter doing what she loves and likes. The young women braided only on Fridays after school and on weekends because she was a full-time learner. She stills braids only on Fridays and weekends because she is now a full time IT student at Rosebank College. The young women really loves braiding.
.Mission and Vision Statements: 
>Mission statement- The salon is designed to make every woman’s crown (hair) to shine and look good.
>Vision statement- The salon is there to inspire woman to take care of their crown (hair). And make woman to always feel and look like princesses and queens.
.Target Audience: The salon is for every type of woman who likes to see their crown(hair) fixed. Also includes man who loves to be braided.


## Website Goals and Objectives

.Specific goals for the website.
>Online bookings- When client books online they must specify when they are braiding, what they are going to braid, and at what time. This saves both the salon and client’s time. When a client have booked there is no need for them to come to the salon just to wait on a queue, they come at the time that they have booked for. It also saves clients money due to distance and food. On the website there must be a “book now” bar to click when booking, it will consider you to fill details. Details that are going to be filled is your name, what you would like to braid, when are you going to braid, and at what time, then you confirm your booking by paying a deposit fee. The deposit is for in case the client don’t come, that is seen as wasting then salons time so is paid to close the gap. 
>Showing of expertise-  The braiders will be posting videos on a website, sharing tips and doing tutorials on how to braid yourself and take care of your hair at home. This could attract new people to come to the salon and give out a trusted relationship  between the salon and clients.  
>Keeping updates- The salon would regularly post information on the website such as new hairstyles, specials and changes about the salon. Consistent updates proves and shows the clients and people that the salon is still active and still exists.
.KIPs.
>Online bookings-  Measures how many clients are coming to the salon to braid. Increased bookings proves that the website is being visited and functions well. For example, 10 bookings increased to 15 bookings in the next month.
>Showing of expertise- When viewers comment on how the tips and tutorials are working for them at home, it shows that people are loving them and trying them at home. This invites more people to the salon. For example, 15 comments increased to 20 comments in a week.
> When likes, views and follower’s increases it shows people and clients recommend our website and salon. This provide growth to the salon. For example, 20 to 50 views, 50 to 100 likes, 300 to 500 followers.
